SMASH TROOPERS QUEUIENG

Smash Trooper is a membership badminton club under the supervision of 4th Infantry Division.This is the current badminton club that Sheen is a member. To be able to join the club, there is a lifetime membership fee of 100 only and you are ready to play. The club is open for all kinds of levels. The members are also friendly on court. The playing venue is located in Pelota Court Camp Evangelista Patag, Cagayan de Oro City. The venue is consists of two courts and is exclusive for play only to the members of the club.

The game schedule is T-TH-S. Tuesday starts at 5pm to 9pm, the same schedule on Thursday and Saturday. The court fee is 50 pesos play all you can till it's time. There is also a shuttlecock fee of 15 pesos per game. The club has also a policy that if the total of person on court has 6 pairs, the majority will proceed on a mini-tournament. The mini-tournament has a pot money of 50 pesos, this will go to the clubs proceeds, 50 pesos for the court fee, and 15 pesos for shuttlecock every game.

To be able to join the club, you can leave a message below to show your interets on joining the club. Thank You!
